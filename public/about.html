Our Story

More than photographs — a living diary.

Campus Gallery started as a simple idea: what if we had one place where every laugh, every late night, every proud moment could live together?

Campus Life · Uganda
Why We Built This
"We built this gallery because memories deserve better than a phone camera roll that nobody ever scrolls back through."

Social media is great for sharing, but terrible for keeping. Posts get buried, accounts get deleted, and suddenly a whole year of memories just… vanishes. Campus Gallery is our answer to that — a dedicated, private, beautifully organised space that belongs to us.

What Makes It Ours

Built with intention,
made for memory.

Private by Default

This gallery is invite-only. Every person here was added intentionally. No strangers, no algorithms — just us.

Every Detail Captured

Each photo remembers who was in it, where it was taken, who took it, and when. Context that photo rolls never give you.

Yours to Keep

Download any photo in the resolution you need — from 720p to 4K. Your memories in full quality, whenever you want them.

Organised by Year

Every semester, every academic year — browsable and searchable. The story of our time on campus, in order.

Personal Collections

Save your favourite photos, create your own categories, and build a personal slice of the gallery that is uniquely yours.

Works Everywhere

Install it on your phone like an app, or access it from any browser. Campus Gallery goes wherever you go.
